My Positions

Speak Your Truth (Unless It's Stupid)

Feel free to complain about your day, your boss, or even the weather. But if your "truth" is a lukewarm take or a rehashed meme, prepare to be publicly clowned.

Karen-Free Zone

If you dare complain about the group rules, I'll personally draft a post highlighting your audacity for everyone to enjoy. You’ve been warned.

Polls for the Pettiest Problems

Think your gripe is the pettiest? Think again. Weekly polls will decide which complaint reigns supreme, and losers will be openly mocked for their weak entries.

The Clapback Clause

If you think you can outwit me in the comments, think again. As moderator, I’ll be serving up spicy clapbacks like it’s happy hour. Bring your best game or don’t play.

Every Post Gets a Response

Think you can post something so bad I won’t respond? Challenge accepted. But fair warning: my response will probably sting.

Lurkers Beware

If you’re here just to scroll and silently judge, know that I will find you, and I will call you out. Contribute or get roasted.
